About Vijay Electronics

Welcome to Vijay Electronics, your number one source for all things related to security and surveillance in Unnao and surrounding districts. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of CCTV installation and repair services, with an emphasis on quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction.

Founded over 5 years ago, Vijay Electronics has come a long way from its beginnings in Unnao. When we first started out, our passion for "Security for Everyone" drove us to start our own business.

We are authorized dealers and installers for major brands like CP Plus, Hikvision, and Dahua. Whether you need a simple home setup or a complex commercial security system, our team of certified technicians is ready to help.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, high-quality, and affordable security solutions to every home and business in Unnao, ensuring peace of mind for our community.
